Tech immigrants outearn most households at $86,164/year

Since 2012 the US tech industry has employed 176,077 foreign nationals. Most of them made between $60,660 and $111,668 per year. The best city to be a techie is Seattle, WA with an average individual salary $36,121 above household median.
